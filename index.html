Hi! I'm Joseph. I'm currently a Junior at Glenbrook South High School in Glenview, Illinois. At school, I am a part of the Stage Crew. We're the people who work behind the scenes of productions such as the plays, Variety Show, and Musical.

Not only do we design and construct the those sets, but we also run the show. We perfect the lighting, sound, and so much more! We strive to make sure that each show runs perfectly and start to finish. After the show, we strike the set and get ready to do it all over again!
